## What Does "All-in-One CRM for Agencies" Actually Mean?

Before we compare tools, let's define the term. A true all-in-one CRM for agencies is a platform that handles *at minimum*:

1. **Contact and pipeline management** — track leads, deals, and client relationships
2. **Marketing automation** — email sequences, SMS drips, task triggers
3. **Landing pages and funnels** — build and host client campaigns
4. **Appointment scheduling** — calendar booking without Calendly add-ons
5. **Multi-client (sub-account) architecture** — manage each client separately under one login
6. **Reporting** — show clients what's working

Most tools hit 3 or 4 of these. Only a few hit all 6. That distinction matters enormously when you're billing 15 clients and need to move fast.
